So what will happen if we don't stop this madness?

Climate Change: This is one of the biggest issues and deforestation is the biggest factor contributing to it. It's affecting the global carbon cycle. This global warming is in turning affecting all living beings!

Soil Erosion: This is second biggest issue which is concerning the environmentalists. This is a major problem as without trees the soil cannot hold its essential nutrients which is why it loses its value.

Water Cycle: This is also being affected by deforestation as trees play a major role in the water cycle.
